Sources

Bible text, location references, and map content used in this reader come from the project's bundled data and the sources below.

  • NIV text: downloaded from aruljohn/Bible-niv and bundled locally in Bible-niv-main.
  • KJV text: downloaded from aruljohn/Bible-kjv and bundled locally in Bible-kjv-main.
  • LXX text: built from the bundled public-domain Brenton English Septuagint USFM source and converted into the local reader format in Bible-lxx-pdf-main.
  • Location references: the place-name index in this repo was rebuilt from Wikipedia: List of biblical places and Wikipedia: List of minor biblical places, then bundled locally in locations.json.
  • Location maps and images: displayed location media is stored locally in img/location; curated historical maps were gathered from Wikimedia Commons, and related media manifests are bundled in location-media.json.
